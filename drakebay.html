Costa Rica - Drake Bay

Our first time visiting Costa Rica was in January 2018 and we visited two locations there, Arenal Volcano and Drake Bay. This post will discuss the Drake Bay area, if you want to learn more about Arenal Volcano click here! We're planning another trip to Costa Rica and it was honestly hard for me to not go back to Drake Bay and stay at the exact same hotel. If I had to pick my absolute favorite location I've ever been to, it would probably be Drake Bay. Costa Rica is a gorgeous country so I really don't think you can go wrong visiting any region, below is some more specific information about the Drake Bay area.


Also we always book our trips through Costa Rica Expeditions who are abosolutely amazing and really make planning these trips easy. I usually do all the planning for our trips but I happened to use them for our first trip to Costa Rica and now I can't imagine doing it any other way.

Drake Bay
  • While a popular destination, Drake Bay is a little more remote. We had to take a small plane from San Jose to get here, then we drove for 10 minutes and hopped on a boat to get to the inn. Because of the difficulty in getting here it's not nearly as crowded as other places.
  • We stayed at Aguila De Osa Inn. I'm sure there are several other nice places to stay in the area but I can't imagine staying anywhere else. Plus they had two adorable dogs that you get to hang out with.
  • One of the major draws of this area is snorkeling/scuba diving in Cano Island. There are a couple different tour groups that do this in the area, usually you can book it from whatever hotel you're staying at.
  • Corcovado National Park is located near Drake Bay and is worth taking multiple trips to see because it's a huge park. Definitely get a tour guide, either a private one or a group tour! We saw a lot more wildlife in this area than we did in Arenal Volcano.
