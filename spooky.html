Spooky Time

I grew up reading Stephen King books and then I found myself living right next to Maine. I can't help but take some horror themed photos sometimes, particulary on foggy nights, I'm morally obligated to do so as an avid horror fan.


These images are a little different from other ones you'll see on my website. They tend to be more heavily edited to really capture the emotion of that particular shoot. Some of the images also feature either myself or whatever friend I can convince to hang out with me at night and be creepy with me.
