Chasing Waterfalls

I complain about living in New England constantly. I've been here almost a decade and I still can't handle winter. I miss a lot of things about California and the west coast. But the one thing I do love about New England is all the waterfalls that are scattered across the region.


While astrophotography is my first love, and you can read all about my obsession with it here, it's not something that's always feasible. But most waterfalls in New England are present year round and it gives me something to do in between planning astrophotography trips. These are some of my favoorite locations I've found so far.

Favorite Locations
  • Buttermilk Falls, NY. This is located right next to the Forked Lake Campgrounds and is worth visiting if you're in the area. There's nothing particulary special about these waterfalls but the Adirondacks in general are worth visiting, there are lots of awesome places in the region including these waterfalls.
  • Green Forest, Vermont.
  • Stowe, Vermont.
